Why we lauch MyBioMarker?

Easier to prevent rather than to treat. We are here to make it happen.

MyBioMarker launched with the mission to help anyone who is experiencing period cramps, PMS, PCOS, endometriosis and other challenging conditions to understand what actually is happening and how to ease and take control of these symptoms.

Uploading results of your pre-made medical tests aims to track essential biomarkers. Our algorithms will notify you on any suspicious deviations in your data so that you get an early possible warning.

  • Why should I use MyBioMarker?
    MyBioMarker is able to do two important things for you:

    - keep your records in one place;

    - train the algorithm based on the medical records you uploaded;

    MyBioMarker helps you to look after early signs of different health conditions.
  • How do you protect my data?
    People trust MyBioMarker to store medical data. We use only trusted services for data storage and only in encrypted and obfuscated way. We make sure that data is encrypted at rest (at client side) and transmission happens only over secure protocol. However, MyBioBot is trained using your data, we never use your personal data or sell it.
