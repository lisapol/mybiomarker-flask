Why we lauch MyBioMarker?

Easier to prevent rather than to treat. We are here to make it happen.

MyBioMarker launched with the mission to help anyone who is experiencing period cramps, PMS, PCOS, endometriosis and other challenging conditions to understand what actually is happening and how to ease and take control of these symptoms.

MyBioMarkers tests are aimed to track essential biomarkers, store your results and calculate your personal reference value. Our algorithms will notify you on any suspicious deviations in your data so that you get an early possible warning.

MyBioMarker is not a medical device. MyBioMarker is a product designed to continuously learn from the collected data to help to understand ovaluation and other patterns of different biomarkers. False positive and False negative readings about health data are potential risk of MyBioMarker. MyBioMarker does not guarantee that health patterns are 100% accurate since all predictions are made based on neural network algorithms. MyBioMarker should never be a substitute to consultations with medical professionals.
